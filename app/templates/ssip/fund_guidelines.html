SSIP Fund Request Submission Process

Submission Process

Step 1 Submit Form 1 (Fund Request) for Approval

Your form will be reviewed by the SSIP Coordinator. Please wait for confirmation from the Coordinator before proceeding with any purchases.

Go to Fund Request Form
Step 2 Submit Form 2 (Utilization Certificate)

Once your purchases are completed, submit Form 2 along with the invoices.

This will be verified and processed by the SSIP Coordinator and Finance Member for final approval.

Go to Utilization Form

After Submission

Form 1 Workflow:
  1. Your request is forwarded to the SSIP Coordinator for review
  2. Wait for confirmation before making any purchases
Form 2 Workflow:
  1. Submit to Department SSIP Coordinator
  2. Wait for verification
  3. Processing by SSIP Finance Member
  4. Final approval by Institute SSIP Coordinator
Important Note

Do not proceed with purchasing any items until you have received confirmation from your SSIP Coordinator after submitting Form 1.
